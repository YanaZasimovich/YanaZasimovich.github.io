About me

I'm a 2D artist and illustrator. I work in the gaming industry. I draw sketches, art concept, illustrations, portraits, mattepainting and others. I love games, movies, and animation, and I love dissecting them while thinking about how to take them to the next level. I have a passion for FromSoftware games and understand their unique approach to creating art for their game universes.

Illustration

My background

At least two years of professional experience as a concept artist and illustrator. I understand the role of the concept artist in the game development process. Extraordinary ability creating traditional 2D art. I know when to work quickly to generate conceptual ideas and when to slow down and polish an image for public consumption. I am highly creative and an expert with design, silhouette, and structure when creating characters, and I am a pro with lighting, staging, color, and composition when developing fun and beautiful environmental art. Understanding of human and creature anatomy. I know how to stylize them to create compelling and memorable characters with feeling and emotion. I love to collaborate. Creating epic entertainment experiences is a team effort and I thrive when exploring ideas and working with others.

Qualifications

Attention to detail and a love of process. Strong understanding of art fundamentals including visual design, shape language, color theory, and lighting. Desire and ability to find creative solutions to artistic challenges and problems. Expert knowledge of Photoshop.
